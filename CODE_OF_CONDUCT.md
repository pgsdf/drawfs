# Code of Conduct

drawfs is a technical project. We aim for a respectful, direct, and constructive culture.

## Expectations

* Be respectful in language and intent.
* Assume good faith, especially during review.
* Keep discussions technical and specific.
* Disagree with ideas, not with people.
* Prefer reproducible reports (logs, traces, test scripts).

## Unacceptable behavior

* Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
* Threats or intimidation
* Repeated disruption of technical discussion
* Deliberate misrepresentation of results

## Reporting

If an interaction becomes inappropriate, contact the project maintainers through the repository issue tracker or the maintainer email listed in the repository metadata.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take actions that include.

* Requesting edits to comments
* Locking threads
* Temporary or permanent bans from project spaces
